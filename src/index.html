Maryland supports child care providers.

The Moore-Miller Administration’s historic investments in child care have supported more families in accessing free or low-cost child care through the Child Care Scholarship program – setting up children for long-term academic success while removing employment barriers for adults and bolstering our state’s economy.

The Administration is also expanding awareness of and access to the Maryland Earned Income Tax Credit and the Maryland Child Tax Credit.

Visit the Start a Child Care Business to join us and collaborate on making a better future for Maryland, our communities and most importantly our children.
